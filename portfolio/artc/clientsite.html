Client Based Website

ohwhale.co

Ohwhale.co is a small shop that is based in Hawai'i and California. The products include kpop fan merchandise and creative and stylized products, such as stickers, pins, and keychains.

My goal was to create a one-stop shop for ohwhale.co which included their own ecommerce platform and account system. I initially created many pages that proved to be more than I could handle, thus I cut back and have four pages remaining.

I pulled from ohwhale.co's original artwork to add adorable graphics around the page, as well as taking inspiration from their Instagram postings for additional organic shapes and symbols.
